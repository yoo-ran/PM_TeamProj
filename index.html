Welcome to Coastal Cove Cafe!

We’re a local Vancouver cafe hoping to bring you all the fun beach vibes, while still serving up your cozy cafe favorites. We’ve been proudly serving you and our community since 2018 and look forward to many more years to come!

Fall Feature: Pumpkin Spice Latte

Here at Coastal Cove Cafe, we’re channeling all the pumpkin-spice-and-all-things-nice vibes! For the months of October and November, you can try our unique pumpkin spice latte made with our in-house pumpkin syrup - you won’t taste another like it anywhere else.

Sing-Along Saturdays

Got weekend plans this fall? Come join us for live music every Saturday! Between 11-4pm, we’ll have local artists share their talent on our patio. Don’t worry - we’ve got the fall cozy vibes so all you’ve got to do is bring some friends and along! If you’re interested in performing, please contact us at contact@coastalcove.ca.
